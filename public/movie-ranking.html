The Best Movies According to Jacob

My Top Three Movies of All Time.

1. The Expendables.

This is my favourite action movies of all time, I loved watching it and I can watch it all the time without getting bored.

2. The Blacklist.

This was an amazing and well acted action series, I watched it until the end keenly and it was very entertaining.

3. Friends.

This is a drama series that is very funny and one can watch it over and over again without getting bored.
